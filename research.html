Research themes

Examples of recent projects:

  • 2022:
    • Neural networks for solving the chemical master equation (2022): Approximate steady-state joint distribution solutions for a chemical master equation two-species model of the life cycle of RNA using neural networks. (preprint).
    • Software tools for genomics (2022): gget for efficient querying of genomic reference databases (preprint) and ffq for metadata retrieval from sequence databases (preprint).
    • Foundations and performance of count normalization methods (2022): A comprehensive benchmark of widely used methods on 526 datasets (preprint); analysis of biological and technical variance and an argument for model based normalization (preprint).
    • Understanding RNA velocity (2022): An in depth examination of the foundations of single-cell RNA velocity and an exposition of a Markovian framework that may ameliorate current problems (preprint).
